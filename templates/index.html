About Me

My name is Denis Mbey Akola. I was born on June 1st,1997. I hail from Kuboko,Zebilla in the Upper East Region of Ghana. I had my basic education in St. Charles Lwanga Junior High School in Zebilla. I attended Saint Charles Minor Seminary SHS in Tamale (2012-2015) where I studied general science. I am currently an undergraduate Student at the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology(KNUST) where I study computer Engineering.I love programming,problem solving ,machine learning and artificial intelligence. I program in C,C++,Python and Java. I am also an online distance learning student with the International College of the Bible(ICOTB) studying an Associate of Arts Degree program in Bible. I am avid reader and writer.
